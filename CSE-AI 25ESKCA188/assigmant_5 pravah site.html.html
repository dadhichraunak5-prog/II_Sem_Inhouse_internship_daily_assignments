About the fest

26 years of Pravah

Pravah is SKIT Jaipur's flagship annual techno-cultural celebration, running alongside its technical counterpart Aaveg. Every year the fest draws thousands of students from across the region for competitions, performances and workshops spanning dance, music, drama, fine arts, fashion and literature.

The Navras — Shringara, Hasya, Karuna, Raudra, Veera, Bhayanaka, Bibhatsya, Adbhuta and Shanta — are the nine emotions outlined in Bharata's Natyashastra, the ancient treatise on performing arts. Indian classical forms like Bharatnatyam, Kathak and Odissi are built around evoking these very rasas in an audience. Pravah 2026 takes this idea as its theme: every stage, every act, every corner of campus becomes an expression of one of these nine emotions.
